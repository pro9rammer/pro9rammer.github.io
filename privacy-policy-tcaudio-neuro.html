Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 14, 2026

tc.audio Neuro is a focus and sleep sound app developed by tc.audio. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

tc.audio Neuro does not collect personally identifiable information. The app does not require account registration, login, email address, name, phone number, or location information.

tc.audio Neuro generates and plays sound locally on your device. The app does not record audio, access your microphone, upload audio, or transmit listening activity to our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The current version of tc.audio Neuro does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer personal data to third parties because the app does not collect personal data.

5. Children’s Privacy

tc.audio Neuro is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Security

Since the app does not collect or store personal information on our servers, there is no personal data transmitted to or stored by us.
