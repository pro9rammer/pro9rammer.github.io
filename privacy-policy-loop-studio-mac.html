Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 18, 2026

tc.audio Loop Studio does not collect, store, or share personal information.

The app processes audio files locally on your Mac device for playback, looping, waveform visualization, pitch shifting, speed adjustment, and practice workflows.

No audio files or personal data are uploaded to external servers.

Information Collection

tc.audio Loop Studio does not:

  • Require account creation
  • Track user activity
  • Collect analytics
  • Share personal information with third parties
  • Upload audio content to cloud servers

File Access

The app may request access to audio files selected by the user in order to:

  • Import audio files
  • Generate waveforms
  • Perform playback and looping features
  • Adjust pitch and playback speed

All processing is performed locally on the user's device.
